    The pillars of productivity are efficiency, accuracy, and focus. The Pomoduro method takes these concepts and helps you form a work flow that will not improve your academics but also give you more time to be you. The Pomodoro technique involves breaking down work into 25-minute intervals (pomodoros) with 5 minute short breaks in between, followed by a longer 15 minute break after four pomodoros.
